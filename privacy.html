FoodSafe UK – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

FoodSafe UK ("we", "us") is operated by Smart Cric Tech. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

Data We Collect

  • Email address and name (when you register)
  • Location data (to find nearby food businesses)
  • Business information (if you register a food business)
  • Photos uploaded for premises compliance records
  • Reviews you write about food businesses

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide food safety compliance tools to business owners
  • To show nearby food business hygiene ratings to consumers
  • To display reviews and ratings on business profiles

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (EU region). We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third Party Services

  • Google Places API – to retrieve business location data
  • Food Standards Agency API – to retrieve hygiene ratings

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing us.
