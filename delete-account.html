FoodSafe UK – Account Deletion Request

To request deletion of your FoodSafe UK account and all associated data, email us at:

tech.cric.smart@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address registered to your account
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and login credentials
  • Any reviews you have written
  • Any business information you have submitted
  • All compliance records associated with your account

Retention period

All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

Some anonymised data (e.g. aggregate review scores) may be retained.
